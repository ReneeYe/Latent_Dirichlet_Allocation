 (CNN)Zlatan Ibrahimovic has never been short of self-belief.
Famous for such lines as: "A World Cup without me is nothing to watch" and "nothing, she already has Zlatan" when asked what he was getting his wife as a birthday gift, the 34-year-old's confidence levels seem to have hit new heights.
Talking to CNN's Alex Thomas, the Sweden star -- who will be playing for his country at the upcoming European Championship -- claimed his is an "11 out of 10" player and an even better person (20 out of 10, apparently.)
